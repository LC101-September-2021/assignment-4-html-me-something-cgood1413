When planning for children, my mother initially wanted an eldest son and a younger daughter. After having my eldest sister for her first child, she decided that she would be content with the inverse: a younger son and eldest daughter. This led to the birth of my second oldest sister. Realizing that her original plan wasn’t meant to be, my mother decided that she simply just wanted a son and so had my third sister. Finally, she told herself that she would try just once more for a boy and regardless of the outcome it would be her last child. The universe then decided to stop playing its cosmic joke with my mother, who had her one and only boy that she wanted; her last Chance for a son.
